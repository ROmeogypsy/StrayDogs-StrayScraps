# Police Response Protocols

## THREAT ASSESSMENT LEVELS

### LEVEL 1: Minor (Noise Complaint)
**Example**: Party in North Ends.
**Response**: Ignore or slow roll.
**Timeline**: Hours (if ever).

### LEVEL 2: Moderate (Property Damage)
**Example**: Vandalism, graffiti.
**Response**: Report filed online. No patrol.
**Timeline**: Days.

### LEVEL 3: Serious (Assault Report)
**Example**: Fight in Plaza.
**Response**: Patrol car sent. Silence Protocol engaged.
**Timeline**: 30-60 minutes.

### LEVEL 4: Critical (Major Violence/Death)
**Example**: Murder, large brawl.
**Response**: Multiple units. Investigation opened.
**Timeline**: Immediate.

### LEVEL 5: Existential (Riot/Terrorism)
**Example**: Massive civil unrest.
**Response**: Riot squad. Media blackout.
**Timeline**: Instant.

## VIOLATION RESPONSES (External)
- **Silence Protocol**: Community refuses to cooperate. No witnesses found.
- **SD Media**: Viral video forces accountability.
