# March 2023: The Catalyst

## Overview
The month everything changed. Death, kidnapping, and the forging of the Dogs as they are now.

## Key Events
-   **Captain Shaw's Death**: Stray's father killed (ambush). Stray witnesses it.
-   **K-9 Kidnapping**: Taken by pimps from the Red Light.
-   **Big 9's Rescue**: Kills three men to save his brother.
-   **The Darkest Light**: Big 9 and Stray record tracks in The Kennel.

## Significance
-   **Stray's Trauma**: Begins his descent into silence.
-   **Big 9's Sacrifice**: The act that will cost him his freedom.
-   **K-9's Burden**: Guilt becomes his driving force.
