# Intelligence Network

## Overview
The "Eyes" of the **Stray Dogs**. Led by **Snout** ("The Vet").

## Components
1.  **Ghost Dogs (SEPH)**: 14 scouts in South Ends. Report rival gang movements.
2.  **Community Eyes**: Shopkeepers, residents, kids, Red Light workers.
3.  **Embedded Agents**: Rax and K-9 at Academy. (Information from "The Hill").
4.  **Snout's Pub**: Marlowe Arms. Snout listens to the community gossip.

## Communication
-   **Burner Phones**: Encrypted messaging (Signal/WhatsApp).
-   **Dead Drops**: Physical notes hidden in specific locations (like Zed's tally marks).
-   **Visual Signals**: Red laces, specific graffiti tags.
-   **Whistle**: Immediate distress signal.

## Significance
-   **Pre-emption**: Stops threats before they reach Level 3 (Probing Attack).
-   **Protection**: Allows 65 Dogs to protect 45,000 people.
