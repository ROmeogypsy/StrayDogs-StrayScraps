# Whistle (The Ears)

## Overview
Age: 16. Intelligence and Observer. **Stray's right hand** in the Red Light. Expert lip-reader. Speaks only when necessary (like Stray, but less haunted).

## Physical Description
-   **Build**: Small, wiry, observant. Often blends into shadows.
-   **Face**: Constantly scanning, quick eyes.
-   **Clothing**: Hood always up. NEPH grey jumpsuit. Red dog head on wrist (small).
-   **Distinctive**: Never misses a whisper. Uses a dog whistle to signal.

## Personality
-   **Observant**: Sees things others miss (details, body language).
-   **Quiet**: Uses silence as a weapon/tool.
-   **Loyal**: Devoted to Stray's methods. "I see what you see."

## Operations
-   **Lip Reader**: Can read conversations across the street/room.
-   **Lookout**: Early warning for patrols (Level 1/2 threats).
-   **Signalman**: Uses whistle codes to alert other Dogs.

## Current Status (Feb 2026)
-   **Red Light**: Often paired with Stray on Graveyard shift.
-   **Scraps**: Observing him closely. Neutral but watchful.
-   **West End**: Providing intel on their scouts.

## Relationships
-   **Stray**: Mentor/idol. Mimics his silence.
-   **Ruckus**: Often paired (brains/eyes + brawn).
-   **Snout**: Feeds intel directly to him.
