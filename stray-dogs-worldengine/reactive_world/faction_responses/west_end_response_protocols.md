# West End Response Protocols

## THREAT ASSESSMENT LEVELS

### LEVEL 1: Minor (Verbal)
**Example**: Dog insults West End member.
**Response**: Threats, show of force.
**Timeline**: Immediate.

### LEVEL 2: Moderate (Loss of Face)
**Example**: West End member humiliated in public.
**Response**: Retaliation (beating, property damage).
**Timeline**: Hours/Days.

### LEVEL 3: Serious (Physical Attack)
**Example**: West End member injured.
**Response**: Organized hit squad (5-10 members).
**Timeline**: Within 24 hours.

### LEVEL 4: Critical (Leader Targeted)
**Example**: Stray disarms Leader.
**Response**: War preparation. All-out assault.
**Timeline**: Weeks (planning).

### LEVEL 5: Existential (Total War)
**Example**: Territory invasion.
**Response**: Total mobilization. Lethal force.
**Timeline**: Instant.

## VIOLATION RESPONSES (Internal)
- **Failure**: Beating.
- **Betrayal (Scraps)**: Death.
