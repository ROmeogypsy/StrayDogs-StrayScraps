# World Index

## Overview
The **Stray Dogs Universe** is set in a fictionalized London borough, the **North Ends**, where systemic neglect has created a power vacuum filled by the **Stray Dogs**, a community-led protection force.

## Key Locations
- **The Plaza**: Neutral ground where four schools converge.
- **North Ends**: Working-class territory of the Dogs.
- **The Doghouse (NEPH)**: Brutalist high school, "prison pipeline".
- **The Kennel**: SD Media HQ and Dog sanctuary.
- **The Hydrant**: Red Light District under Dog protection.

## Key Factions
- **Stray Dogs**: 65 members dedicated to protecting the vulnerable.
- **St. Augustine's Academy**: Elite prep school overlooking the North Ends.
- **West End Gang**: Wealthy rivals threatening the community.
- **Metropolitan Police**: Absent except when inconvenient.
