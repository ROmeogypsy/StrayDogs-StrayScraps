# Physical Mannerisms Guide

## STRAY

**Signature Movements**:
-   **Headphone Adjustment**: Left hand to ear cup (unconscious, 10-15 min, protective).
-   **Lip-Reading Scan**: Eyes dart between mouths (tracking multiple conversations).
-   **Tactical Positioning**: Back to walls, knows exits. Never sits exposed.

**Stress Indicators**:
-   **Hand Tremor**: Fine shake when holding things (caffeine/exhaustion).
-   **Jaw Clench**: Tinnitus spike. Winces slightly.
-   **Stillness**: Freezes like a statue ("The Gargoyle").

**Combat Tells**:
-   **Weight Shift**: Forward onto toes.
-   **Hands Loose**: At sides, ready to grab.
-   **Eyes Narrow**: Focusing on a threat.

## SCRAPS

**Signature Movements**:
-   **Knuckle Crack**: Flexes fingers before action (habit).
-   **Checking Six**: Constantly looking over shoulder (paranoid).
-   **Touching Tattoo**: Fingers trace the dagger behind his ear.

**Stress Indicators**:
-   **Pacing**: Can't stand still.
-   **Fists Ball**: Unconscious aggression.
-   **Voice Crack**: Strain from holding back rage/fear.

**Combat Tells**:
-   **Chin Tuck**: Boxer's stance.
-   **Shoulders Hunch**: Protecting vital areas.
-   **Breathing**: Controlled rhythm (inhale, strike).

## RUCKUS

**Signature Movements**:
-   **Chest Puff**: Expands to look bigger.
-   **Loud Laugh**: Booming, takes up space.
-   **Shoving**: Physical contact to assert dominance.

**Stress Indicators**:
-   **Face Reddens**: anger visible immediately.
-   **Veins Pop**: Neck muscles tense.
-   **Spitting**: Disgust/aggression.

**Combat Tells**:
-   **Roar**: Vocalizes before striking.
-   **Wild Swings**: Over-commits to punches.
-   **Tunnel Vision**: Focuses on one target (Scraps).
