# Late 2023: The Breaking Point

## Overview
Six months after the Catalyst, the consequences hit all at once. The **Stray Dogs** nearly collapse.

## Key Events
-   **September**: Big 9 arrested for the murders (March).
-   **Sentencing**: Big 9 receives **Whole Life Order**. No parole.
-   **One Week Later**: **Zed** dragged by rivals (face/back destroyed). Power vacuum attack.
-   **Weeks Later**: **Ash** (Red Light worker) killed on Stray's watch.
    *   **2:47 AM**.
    *   **Three Doors Down** from Stray.
-   **Stray's Silence**: After Ash, Stray stops speaking. Tinnitus locks catastrophic.

## Significance
-   **Trauma Peak**: Every leader is broken or gone.
-   **Survival**: Dame holds it together by sheer will.
-   **Transformation**: Zed becomes the scarred strategist. Stray becomes the Silent Sentinel.
