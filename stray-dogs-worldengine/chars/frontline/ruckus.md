# Ruckus (The Bark)

## Overview
Age: 17. The Enforcer and "Voice" of the NEPH Frontline. Loud, aggressive, physically strong. Hospitalized by **Scraps** (Harper Whitmore) for 3 weeks in late 2025. Hates him with a passion.

## Physical Description
-   **Build**: Thick, muscular, built like a linebacker.
-   **Face**: Often scowling, broken nose (Scraps did that).
-   **Clothing**: NEPH grey jumpsuit, sleeves torn off to show muscles/tattoos. Red dog head painted on chest.
-   **Distinctive**: Loud, booming voice. Always ready to fight.

## Personality
-   **Loyal**: Fiercely protective of NEPH Dogs ("The Pack").
-   **Aggressive**: Violence is his first language. "Bark then bite."
-   **Resentful**: Still humiliated by Scraps' beatdown. Cannot forgive the betrayal.

## Operations
-   **Enforcer**: Handles physical threats (Level 1/2).
-   **Patrol Leader**: Often leads NEPH shifts.
-   **Intimidator**: Keeps minor gangs/troublemakers in line.

## Current Status (Feb 2026)
-   **Anti-Scraps**: Openly hostile. Leading NEPH resistance to his integration.
-   **West End**: Wants to fight them ("Bring it on").
-   **Chew-toy**: Respects him but pushes boundaries.

## Relationships
-   **Scraps**: Enemy #1.
-   **Stray**: Respects his silence/skill (even if he scares him).
-   **Matches**: Often partnered (chaos + muscle).
-   **Whistle**: Protective of the quiet one.
