# Cipher (The Strategist)

Age: 17. **SD Media's** Digital Manager. Handles the algorithms, YouTube analytics, and online presence. Quiet, tech-savvy.

## Physical Description
-   **Build**: Small, pale (lives in the dark), fidgety hands.
-   **Face**: Often hidden behind a screen/monitor.
-   **Clothing**: Oversized hoodies (usually black), fingerless gloves.
-   **Distinctive**: Constantly typing or swiping.

## Personality
-   **Analytical**: Sees the world in data and trends.
-   **Quiet**: Speaks in "codes" or memes often.
-   **Protective**: Guards the digital identity of the Dogs (security).

## Operations

-   **Viral Strategy**: Times releases for maximum impact ("Muzzle Up, Teeth Down").
-   **Hacking**: Basic skills (can access school networks, etc.).
