# SD Media

## Overview
Independent grime label and the **Stray Dogs**' public face. Located in **The Kennel** (47 Marlowe St basement). Use music, videos, and social media to amplify the Dog philosophy ("Lead by Doing").

## Core Members
-   **K-9**: "The Voice". Rapper, Big 9's brother, face tattoos, bisexual. Handles the charisma.
-   **Dame**: "The Matriarch". Videographer, artist. Controls the visual brand.
-   **Prophet**: Lyricist, philosophical voice. Writes the hooks that stick.
-   **Cipher**: Digital manager, algorithms, YouTube strategy.

## Content
