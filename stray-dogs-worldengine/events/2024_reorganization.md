# 2024: The Reorganization

## Overview
The **Stray Dogs** rebuild. **Zed** and **Dame** establish a new order to function without Big 9.

## Key Events
-   **Zed's Rise**: Becomes Underboss candidate. Runs strategy.
-   **K-9's Voice**: SD Media takes off. "Muzzle Up, Teeth Down" goes viral.
-   **Recruitment**: Grows to 64 members. NEPH Frontline formalized.
-   **Chew-toy**: Promoted to Operations Manager.

## Significance
-   **Sustainability**: Proving they are not just Big 9's cult of personality.
-   **Public Face**: K-9 gives them a voice beyond the North Ends.
