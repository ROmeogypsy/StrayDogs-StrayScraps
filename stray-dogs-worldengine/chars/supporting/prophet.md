# Prophet (The Lyricist)

## Overview
Age: 18. **SD Media's** primary lyricist and philosophical voice. Not a fighter, but a poet. Writes the hooks that stick ("Muzzle Up, Teeth Down").

## Physical Description
-   **Build**: Slender, thoughtful posture.
-   **Face**: Often wears headphones or reading glasses.
-   **Clothing**: Simple streetwear, often with notebooks sticking out of pockets.
-   **Distinctive**: Always writing.

## Personality
-   **Deep Thinker**: Contemplates the "why" of the Dogs.
-   **Voice of Reason**: Calming presence in SD Media disputes.
-   **Creative**: Translates Dog philosophy into viral rhymes.

## Operations
-   **Songwriting**: Collaborates with K-9 and Dame.
-   **Documentation**: Chronicles Dog history (unofficial historian).

## Current Status (Feb 2026)
-   **Creative Block**: Struggling with K-9's darker direction.
-   **Project**: Working on a track about Scraps ("Traitor/Savior").
-   **Big 9**: Idolizes his lyrics ("The Darkest Light").

## Relationships
-   **K-9**: Creative partner. Often clashes on tone.
-   **Dame**: Respects her vision.
-   **Cipher**: Collaborator on digital strategy.
