## **PART 5: WRITING CHALLENGES & SOLUTIONS**

### **COMMON PITFALLS TO AVOID**

#### **PITFALL 1: "Too Many Coincidences"**

**Problem**: World feels contrived if everything connects too neatly.

**Examples of BAD COINCIDENCE**:
‚ùå Stray's mother is ALSO connected to West End gang
‚ùå Alistair's father is judge on Big 9's case
‚ùå Scraps' blade tattoo artist is Dame's mentor
‚ùå Every character has trauma connected to same event

**SOLUTIONS**:
‚úÖ Some things are random (Ash's death was random client, not conspiracy)
‚úÖ Keep connection degrees separated (if A connects to B, don't also connect B to C to A)
‚úÖ Let some mysteries be simple (Havenwood could just be financial fraud, not conspiracy)
‚úÖ Background characters stay background (not everyone is secretly related)

---

#### **PITFALL 2: "Trauma Disappears"**

**Problem**: Characters "get over" trauma too quickly or completely.

**Examples of BAD TRAUMA HANDLING**:
‚ùå Stray starts speaking normally after one therapy session
‚ùå Zed's scars magically fade or become "sexy" instead of horrifying
‚ùå Dame stops grieving Big 9 after meeting someone new
‚ùå Scraps is instantly forgiven because he apologized

**SOLUTIONS**:
‚úÖ Trauma integrates, doesn't disappear (Stray might speak MORE, but never chatty)
‚úÖ Scars stay visible (Zed's face is DESTROYED, that's permanent)
‚úÖ Grief is ongoing (Dame loves Big 9 forever, even if life continues)
‚úÖ Forgiveness is earned slowly (Ruckus might forgive eventually, but it takes months)

---

#### **PITFALL 3: "Power Creep"**

**Problem**: Characters become unrealistically skilled/powerful over time.

**Examples of BAD POWER CREEP**:
‚ùå Stray can suddenly defeat 20 enemies alone
‚ùå K-9's YouTube channel gets 10 million followers overnight
‚ùå Dogs acquire military-grade weapons from nowhere
‚ùå Rax's code-switching includes fluent Mandarin now (no explanation)

**SOLUTIONS**:
‚úÖ Skills improve gradually (Stray trains, gets SLIGHTLY better over months)
‚úÖ Success is realistic (SD Media grows to 500k followers over years, not millions overnight)
‚úÖ Resources match income (Dogs can't afford what they can't afford)
‚úÖ New skills require learning (if Rax learns Mandarin, show him studying for months)

---

#### **PITFALL 4: "Conflict Evaporation"**

**Problem**: Tensions resolve too easily, drama disappears.

**Examples of BAD CONFLICT RESOLUTION**:
‚ùå West End gang just forgives Scraps and moves on
‚ùå Ruckus instantly stops being angry after one conversation
‚ùå Academy stops caring about Dogs with no explanation
‚ùå All Dogs immediately accept Scraps because Stray said so

**SOLUTIONS**:
‚úÖ Conflicts have consequences (West End war lasts sessions, has costs)
‚úÖ Emotions are realistic (Ruckus stays angry, forgiveness takes half a year)
‚úÖ Institutional problems persist (Academy/NEPH divide doesn't disappear)
‚úÖ Not everyone agrees (some Dogs reject Scraps permanently, that's valid)

---
