## CORE PHYSICAL DESCRIPTION

### Build & Stature
-   **Height**: 5'9"
-   **Build**: Wiry, thin, hunched shoulders (bad back).
-   **Posture**: Relaxed, unassuming. Often leaning against a bar or wall.

### Face & Head
**Shape & Structure**: Weathered, lined. "A map of the North Ends."
**Eyes**: Warm, brown, crinkled at corners. Hide a sharp intellect.
**Hair**: Greying, thinning.
**Skin**: Tanned/weather-beaten.
**Facial Hair**: Grey stubble/short beard.

### Distinctive Features
1.  **Glasses**: Thick, horn-rimmed. Constantly cleaning them.
2.  **Smell**: Tobacco, stale ale, and peppermint (to hide the tobacco).
3.  **Hands**: Shake slightly, but steady when holding a pint.

## CLOTHING & APPEARANCE

### Primary Outfit
-   **Old School**: Flat cap (tweed), heavy wool coat (grey/brown), fingerless gloves.
-   **Practical**: Layers for warmth.

### Mannerisms
-   **Nursing a Pint**: His default state at the Marlowe Arms.
-   **Winking**: Uses it to signal safety or a joke.
-   **Coughing**: Chronic smoker's cough.
