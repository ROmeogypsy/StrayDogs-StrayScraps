# February 2026: Scraps Claimed

## Overview
**Stray** claims **Scraps** (former West End Prince Harper Whitmore) as the 65th Dog.

## Key Events
-   **The Chase**: Stray hunts Harper for hours.
-   **The Confrontation**: On **TIMTIMS Rooftop**.
-   **The Choice**: Stray puts his own shredded coat on Harper. "Stray claimed him. That's enough."
-   **Zed's Acceptance**: Zed adds the 65th tally mark.
-   **High Command**: Conditionally approves. Ruckus and NEPH Dogs furious.

## Significance
-   **Unprecedented**: An enemy becomes a Dog.
-   **Conflict**: West End declares war (Retaliation).
-   **Hope**: Radical Mercy in action.
