# Chew-toy (The Anchor)

## Overview
Age: 18. Operations Manager and NEPH Dog Coordinator. The "middle management" who makes sure the shifts are filled and the kids are safe.

## Physical Description
-   **Build**: Tall (6'3"), wiry, surprisingly strong.
-   **Face**: Young, open, tired eyes.
-   **Clothing**: Neat grey jumpsuit, practical boots. Always carries a clipboard/tablet.
-   **Distinctive**: Constantly checking his phone/watch (managing 30 teenagers).

## Personality
-   **Reliable**: The glue that holds the daily grind together.
-   **Protective**: Sees the NEPH Dogs as his responsibility.
-   **Stress Magnet**: Takes on everyone's problems so Dame doesn't have to.

## Operations
-   **Shift Coordinator**: Assigns morning/afternoon/graveyard shifts.
-   **Logistics**: Ensures safehouses are stocked, WAG ON has gas, phones are charged.
-   **Mediator**: Handles minor squabbles between Ruckus/Matches/Whistle.

## Current Status (Feb 2026)
-   **Burnout Warning**: Carrying too much. Dame relies on him heavily.
-   **Scraps**: Worried about Ruckus' reaction. Trying to keep peace in NEPH.
-   **West End**: Fears for his "kids" (NEPH frontline) against a real gang.

## Relationships
-   **Dame**: Boss/Mother figure.
-   **Snout**: Mentor (learning intel/community management).
-   **Ruckus**: Often has to rein him in.
-   **Matches**: Clean up his messes (literal/figurative).
