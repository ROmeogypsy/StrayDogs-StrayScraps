# Stray (The Silent Sentinel)

## Overview
Age: 17. The graveyard shift protector. Silent, tactical, and deeply traumatized. He speaks 1-3 words at a time. Lives on the streets (Plaza Bench) or in The Kennel. He is the embodiment of "Lead by Doing".

## Physical Description
-   **Build**: Lean, wiry muscle. Military posture.
-   **Face**: Haunted. Bloodshot eyes from exhaustion.
-   **Clothing**: Grey NEPH jumpsuit with a faded red paw print over the heart ("Sentinel").
-   **Distinctive**: Always wears large, noise-canceling headphones (medical necessity for tinnitus).

## Personality
-   **Silent**: Stops speaking after Ash's death. Uses sign, nods, or single words.
-   **Protective**: Pathological need to save everyone. "Not three doors."
-   **Traumatized**: Tinnitus screams like a raid siren when stressed.
-   **Merciful**: Gave his coat to Harper (Scraps), saving his enemy.

## Combat Style (Tier 1 - Elite)
-   **Disarmament Expert**: Can strip a weapon in seconds.
-   **Defensive**: Uses opponent's momentum. "Muzzle Up, Teeth Down."
-   **Lip Reader**: Can read conversations from across the Plaza.

## Current Status (Feb 2026)
-   **Burnout**: Working 4 graveyard shifts/week. Near collapse.
-   **Mentor**: Training Scraps on the rooftops.
-   **Haunted**: Visits the spot where Ash died every night.

## Relationships
-   **Big 9**: Spiritual father. Visits the prison wall.
-   **Dame**: Mother figure. She tattoos him.
-   **Scraps**: His project. He claimed him.
-   **Ash**: The failure that drives him.
