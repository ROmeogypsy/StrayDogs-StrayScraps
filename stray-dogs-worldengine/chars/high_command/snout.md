# Snout (The Vet)

## Overview
Age: 45. Intelligence Coordinator and Community Liaison. Oldest Dog (Co-founder). Runs the "Snouts Network" (eyes everywhere in North/South Ends).

## Physical Description
-   **Build**: Wiry, grizzled, hunched (bad back).
-   **Face**: Weathered, lined, kind eyes behind thick glasses.
-   **Clothing**: Worn flat cap, heavy coat, always smells of tobacco and ale.
-   **Distinctive**: Always at the Marlowe Arms Pub, nursing a pint (intelligence hub).

## Personality
-   **Wise**: "Old Man Snout" knows everyone's grandmother.
-   **Patient**: Listens more than he talks.
-   **Pragmatic**: Knows the streets don't forgive mistakes.
-   **Ghost Leader**: Coordinates the SEPH Ghost Dogs (scouts).

## Operations
-   **Intel Hub**: Collects rumors, tips, and warnings from community.
-   **Recruiter**: Identify potential new Dogs (quietly).
-   **Connector**: Links the Dogs to the older generation (shopkeepers, grandmothers).

## Current Status (Feb 2026)
-   **Conciliator**: Trying to bridge the gap between NEPH Dogs and High Command regarding Scraps.
-   **Worried**: Sees the West End threat escalating.
-   **Legacy**: Knows he's getting old for the frontline (never was a fighter).

## Relationships
-   **Big 9**: Respected peer.
-   **Dame**: Trusted advisor.
-   **Chew-toy**: Mentoring him to take over operations.
-   **Ghost Dogs**: Their direct handler.
