# Academy Response Protocols

## THREAT ASSESSMENT LEVELS

### LEVEL 1: Minor (Student Infraction)
**Example**: Littering, uniform violation.
**Response**: Prefect citation.
**Timeline**: Immediate.

### LEVEL 2: Moderate (Disruptive Behavior)
**Example**: Noise, minor argument.
**Response**: Detention. Head Boy/Girl involved.
**Timeline**: Within 24 hours.

### LEVEL 3: Serious (Physical Altercation)
**Example**: Fight in Plaza.
**Response**: Suspension. Police called (if necessary).
**Timeline**: Immediate.

### LEVEL 4: Critical (Public Scandal)
**Example**: Viral video of student misconduct.
**Response**: Expulsion. PR damage control.
**Timeline**: Hours.

### LEVEL 5: Existential (Direct Threat to Academy)
**Example**: Riot on campus.
**Response**: Police lockdown. Private security mobilized.
**Timeline**: Instant.

## VIOLATION RESPONSES (Internal)
- **Academic**: Detention/Probation.
- **Social**: Shunning (Nala's domain).
- **Physical**: Expulsion.
