# Action Consequence Tree

## 1. VIOLENCE (Dog vs. Enemy)
-   **Win (Clean)**: Enemy retreats. Rumor spreads (Fear). Reputation +1 (Protector).
-   **Win (Messy)**: Enemy hospitalized. Police called (Silence Protocol tested). Rumor spreads (Brutality).
-   **Loss**: Dog injured. Morale drops. Enemy emboldened (Level up).

## 2. VIOLENCE (Dog vs. Civilian)
-   **Accidental**: Apology required immediately. Silence Protocol at risk.
-   **Intentional**: Immediate suspension (Level 3 Violation). Community backlash.

## 3. PROTECTION (Red Light)
-   **Success**: Worker safe. Trust deepens. Donation received.
-   **Failure**: Worker harmed. Trust shattered. High Command crisis.

## 4. EXPOSURE (Identity Revealed)
-   **To Police**: Arrest warrant issued. Dog goes underground.
-   **To Academy**: Expulsion risk (Rax/K-9). Embedded network lost.
-   **To West End**: Targeted assassination attempt.

## 5. SCRAPS (Integration)
-   **Saves a Dog**: Ruckus hesitates. Trust builds slowly.
-   **Fails/Flees**: Confirmed traitor. Hunted by both sides.
