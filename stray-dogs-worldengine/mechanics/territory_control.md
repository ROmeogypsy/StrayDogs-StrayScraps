# Territory Control

## Overview
How the **Stray Dogs** maintain control over the **North Ends**.

## Core Territory
-   **North Ends**: 45,000 residents. NEPH, The Kennel, The Hydrant. Fully secured.
-   **Patrols**: 30 NEPH Frontline Dogs (Ruckus, Matches, Whistle).
-   **Method**: High visibility, "Lead by Doing".

## Neutral Ground
-   **The Plaza**: Shared with Academy, West End, South Ends. No fighting.
-   **Surveillance**: Academy monitors ("The Hill").
-   **Stray's Bench**: His personal space, respected by all (even rivals, usually).

## Buffer Zones
-   **South Ends**: SEPH Ghost Dogs (scouts). No active control, just observation.
-   **West End**: Hostile territory. No Dogs allowed (Scraps' old home).

## Significance
-   **Defense**: Dogs defend North Ends borders (Level 3/4 threats).
-   **Expansion**: No active expansion plan. Focus on consolidation.
