# Scraps Integration Tension

## Overview
**Scraps** (formerly Harper Whitmore, West End Prince) has been conditionally accepted into the Dogs as the 65th member. This has caused a massive internal rift.

## The Conflict
-   **Pro-Scraps**: High Command (Zed sees potential, Dame sees redemption, Stray claimed him).
-   **Anti-Scraps**: NEPH Dogs (led by Ruckus, whom Scraps hospitalized for 3 weeks).
-   **Reasoning**: "He hurt us. He's rich. He's a traitor." vs "He saved Stray. He has nowhere else. He can be transformed."
