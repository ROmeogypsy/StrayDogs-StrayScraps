# Needles (The Medic)

## Overview
Age: 16. Trauma Specialist. **The Medic** of the Stray Dogs. Saved **Zed**'s life after the drag (kept him breathing until proper care). Known for stitching wounds and calming panic.

## Physical Description
-   **Build**: Small, delicate, precise hands.
-   **Face**: Often looks too young for what she sees.
-   **Clothing**: NEPH grey jumpsuit with a large red cross on the chest. Always carries a medic bag.
-   **Distinctive**: Steady hands, even under pressure.

## Personality
-   **Kind**: "The healer." A gentle contrast to Ruckus/Matches.
-   **Resilient**: Sees the worst injuries (Zed, Ash) and doesn't flinch.
-   **Traumatized**: Still sees Zed's face in nightmares.

## Operations
-   **Field Medicine**: Stitches, bandages, painkillers, CPR.
-   **Trauma Response**: First on scene for severe injuries (Level 3/4 threats).
-   **Clinic**: Often works out of The Kennel or Red Light safehouses.

## Current Status (Feb 2026)
-   **Overworked**: Handling minor injuries from patrols constantly.
-   **Zed**: Protective of him. Feels responsible for his scars.
-   **Stray**: Worried about his burnout. Trying to force him to sleep/eat.

## Relationships
-   **Zed**: Patient/Brother figure.
-   **Stray**: Protective of his health.
-   **Matches**: Often patches him up after he burns himself.
-   **Whistle**: Quiet solidarity.
