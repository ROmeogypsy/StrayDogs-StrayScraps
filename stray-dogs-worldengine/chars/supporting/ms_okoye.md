# Ms. Okoye (NEPH Welfare)

## Overview
Age: 40s. **NEPH Welfare Coordinator**. Overworked, underfunded, and fiercely protective of her students. Knows the **Stray Dogs** are the only ones keeping them safe.

## Physical Description
-   **Build**: Comfortable, motherly but tough.
-   **Face**: Kind, tired eyes. Glasses on a chain.
-   **Clothing**: Professional but practical (cardigans, sensible shoes).
-   **Distinctive**: Always has snacks/resources for hungry kids.

## Personality
-   **Frustrated**: Knows the system ("The Grey") is failing her students.
-   **Pragmatic**: Works with the Dogs because she has no other choice.
-   **Secret Ally**: Provides safe spaces/resources (unofficially).

## Operations
-   **Food Bank**: Runs a small pantry in her office.
-   **Safe Haven**: Allows Dogs to sleep in the library/office if desperate.
-   **Advocate**: Fights administration for resources/funding.

## Current Status (Feb 2026)
-   **Stray**: Worried about him. Knows he's homeless. Tries to help (food/clothes).
-   **Scraps**: Observing the tension. Worried about violence in school.

## Relationships

-   **Chew-toy**: Her main contact for coordinating student needs.
-   **Snout**: Old friends (community network).
