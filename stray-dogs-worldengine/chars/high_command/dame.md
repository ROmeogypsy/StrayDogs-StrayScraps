# Dame (The Matriarch)

## Overview
Age: 27. Operations Leader, Videographer, and Tattoo Artist. The "Mother" of the Dogs. Holds the organization together with steel will and Russian curses.

## Physical Description
-   **Build**: Sharp, angular, commanding presence.
-   **Face**: Piercing blue eyes, cheekbones you could cut glass on.
-   **Clothing**: Black combat boots, layers of dark clothing, always has a camera bag.
-   **Distinctive**: Hands stained with ink. Tattoos visible on neck/wrists.

## Personality
-   **Strict**: Tolerates no incompetence. "Gospodi, durak."
-   **Protective**: Fiercely loyal. Would burn the world for her Dogs.
-   **Artistic**: Records the Dogs' actions ("The Darkest Light").
-   **Grieving**: Missing Big 9 every second, but refuses to break.

## Operations
-   **Coordinator**: Assigns shifts, manages resources, tattoos new members.
-   **Media**: Directs K-9's videos. Controls the narrative.

## Current Status (Feb 2026)
-   **Burdened**: Carrying the weight of leadership alone.
-   **Visits**: Weekly trips to Wormwood Scrubs to see Big 9.
-   **Scraps**: Tentatively supports his integration (sees redemption potential).

## Relationships
-   **Big 9**: Eternal love. Waiting for him.
-   **Stray**: Protective of him. Tattoos him personally.
-   **K-9**: Keeps him focused on the mission, not just fame.
-   **Zed**: Relies on his strategic mind since the drag.
