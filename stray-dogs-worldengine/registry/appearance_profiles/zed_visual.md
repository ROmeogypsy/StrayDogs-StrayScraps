# ZED (The Zombie) - Complete Visual Profile

**Last Updated**: 2026-02-06
**Canon Status**: Scarred / Underboss

---

## CORE PHYSICAL DESCRIPTION

### Build & Stature
-   **Height**: 5'11"
-   **Build**: Thin, slightly hunched (back pain from drag).
-   **Walk**: Slight limp (left leg).

### Face & Head
**Shape & Structure**: Ruined. Left side is a landscape of keloid scars.
**Eyes**: Right eye clear/sharp. Left eye milky/clouded (blind).
**Hair**: Shaved on left side (scar tissue prevents growth), messy on right.
**Skin**: Pale where not scarred.

### Distinctive Features
1.  **The Scars**: Face, neck, and entire back. Asphalt burns from the drag.
2.  **Chalk Dust**: Red dust often under fingernails or on clothes.
3.  **Mask**: Often wears a gaiter or scarf to cover lower face in public.

## CLOTHING & APPEARANCE

### Primary Outfit
-   **Concealing**: Oversized hoodies (hood up), scarves.
-   **Colors**: Dark grey, black.
-   **Z-Artist Gear**: When "working", wears a specific paint-splattered coat.

## MANNERISMS
-   **Touching Face**: Rarely. Avoids contact with scars.
-   **The Stare**: Uses his good eye to drill into people.
-   **Silence**: Speaks even less than before. "Simple."

## HOW TO DESCRIBE ZED IN SCENES

### First Appearance:
