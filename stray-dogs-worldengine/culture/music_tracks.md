# Music Tracks (SD Media)

## 1. THE DARKEST LIGHT
-   **Artist**: Big 9 (feat. Stray).
-   **Recorded**: March 2023 (Pre-Prison).
-   **Vibe**: Haunting, slow burn, prophetic.
-   **Lyrics**: *"Three doors down. Three lives lost. Who pays the cost?"*
-   **Significance**: The founding anthem. Stray's only recorded vocals.

## 2. MUZZLE UP, TEETH DOWN
-   **Artist**: K-9.
-   **Recorded**: 2024 (Post-Prison).
-   **Vibe**: Aggressive grime, 140 BPM, mosh pit energy.
-   **Lyrics**: *"Muzzle up, teeth down. North Ends wear the crown. Police look around? Not a sound."*
-   **Significance**: Viral hit. Defined the Dog attitude for the new generation.

## 3. SILENCE PROTOCOL
-   **Artist**: Prophet (feat. K-9).
-   **Recorded**: 2025.
-   **Vibe**: Spoken word over heavy bass.
-   **Lyrics**: *"They ask for names. We give them silence. They ask for blame. We give them silence."*
-   **Significance**: Teaches the community how to resist police questioning.

## 4. RED MARKINGS
-   **Artist**: K-9.
-   **Recorded**: Early 2026 (Unreleased).
-   **Vibe**: Angry, fast, diss track aimed at West End.
-   **Lyrics**: *"Designer clothes don't make you hard. Come to the yard. Leave with a scar."*
-   **Significance**: Escalating tension with West End.
