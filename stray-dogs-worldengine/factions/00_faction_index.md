# Faction Index

## STRAY DOGS (The 65)
Community-led protection force. "Lead by Doing".
-   **High Command**: Big 9 (Founder, imprisoned), Dame (Ops), Snout (Intel), Zed (Strategy), Chew-toy (Manager).
-   **Frontline (NEPH)**: 30 enforcers (Ruckus, Whistle, Matches, Needles).
-   **Embedded**: Agents in Academy (Rax, K-9).
-   **Ghosts (SEPH)**: 14 scouts (intelligence network).
-   **SD Media**: Voice of the Dogs (K-9, Dame, Prophet, Cipher).
-   **New Member**: Scraps (conditional).

## ST. AUGUSTINE'S ACADEMY
Elite prep school. "Scientia et Virtus".
-   **Prefects**: Student enforcement (Alistair, Nala, Leo).
-   **Administration**: Ignorant or complicit in inequality.

## RIVALS
-   **West End Gang**: Wealthy, organized, expanding.
-   **South Ends Gangs**: Fragmented but dangerous.

## LAW ENFORCEMENT
-   **Metropolitan Police**: Absent by design (Silence Protocol).
-   **HM Protective Services**: Involved in Stray's sealed records.
