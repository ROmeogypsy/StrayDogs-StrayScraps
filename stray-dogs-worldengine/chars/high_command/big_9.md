# Big 9 (The Founder)

## Overview
Age: 28. Founder and Spiritual Leader of the Stray Dogs. Currently serving a **Whole Life Order** at **Wormwood Scrubs** for killing three pimps to save his brother, K-9.

## Physical Description
-   **Build**: Massive, broad shoulders.
-   **Face**: Calm, fatherly, tattooed ("Lead by Doing" on neck).
-   **Clothing**: Prison grey (ironically matches NEPH jumpsuits).
-   **Distinctive**: Deep, resonant voice (recorded on "The Darkest Light").

## Personality
-   **Philosopher**: Believes actions > words. "We don't ask. We do."
-   **Selfless**: Sacrificed his freedom without hesitation for family.
-   **Guiding Light**: Even from prison, his letters/messages guide the Dogs.

## Combat Style (Tier 1 - Elite)
-   **Powerhouse**: Brutal strength, street brawler.
-   **Restrained**: Only uses violence when absolutely necessary (Level 4/5 threats).

## Current Status (Feb 2026)
-   **Imprisoned**: Visits from Dame weekly. Writes letters.
-   **Symbol**: His cell is a shrine to the cause. Other prisoners respect him.
-   **Legacy**: The Dogs struggle to maintain unity without him physically there.

## Relationships
-   **Dame**: Soulmate. 75 years apart, but connected.
-   **K-9**: Little brother. Protects him from inside.
-   **Stray**: Spiritual son. Sees himself in Stray's silence.
-   **Snout**: Old friend, co-founder.
