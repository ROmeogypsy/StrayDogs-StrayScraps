# Nala Regan (Head Girl)

## Overview
Age: 17. Head Girl/Vice President of **St. Augustine's Academy**. The "Gossip Queen" who knows everyone's business. Runs the Instagram account `@AugustinesInsider`.

## Physical Description
-   **Build**: Petite, perfectly put together.
-   **Face**: Wide, expressive eyes, always smiling (calculated).
-   **Clothing**: Academy uniform tailored to perfection. Expensive accessories.
-   **Distinctive**: Always on her phone.

## Personality
-   **Inquisitive**: Needs to know everything. Secrets are currency.
-   **Theorist**: Convinced Rax and K-9 are secretly dating ("Boyfriend Theory").
-   **Socialite**: Navigates the social hierarchy of "The Hill" effortlessly.

## Operations
-   **Information Broker**: If something happens at the Academy, Nala knows first.
-   **Prefect**: Enforces rules, but mostly uses the position for access.
-   **Influencer**: Controls the narrative of the student body.

## Current Status (Feb 2026)
-   **Investigating**: Digging into Rax's background (why does he disappear?).
-   **Campaign**: Trying to prove her Rax/K-9 theory.
-   **Alistair**: Finds his moral crisis boring but dangerous to order.

## Relationships
-   **Alistair**: Work husband (Head Boy/Girl dynamic).
-   **Rax**: "Bestie" (she thinks).
-   **Leo**: Finds him crude but useful muscle.
