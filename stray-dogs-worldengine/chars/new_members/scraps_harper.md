# Scraps / Harper Whitmore (The 65th Dog)

## Overview
Age: 17. The **65th Dog**. Formerly **Harper Whitmore**, the "Prince" of the West End Gang. Betrayed his crew to save Stray. Now hunted by his former life. Conditionally accepted by the Dogs.

## Physical Description
-   **Build**: Lean, fighter's build (bare-knuckle boxer).
-   **Face**: Handsome, sharp jawline (Theo Brady-esque), but bruised/battered.
-   **Clothing**: Expensive designer streetwear (ruined/dirty) or borrowed Dog gear.
-   **Distinctive**: **Blade Tattoo** behind right ear (pointing down = killed for the set). Scarred knuckles.

## Personality
-   **Desperate**: Has lost everything (wealth, status, home). Fighting for a new life.
-   **Violent**: Raised to hurt people. Learning to protect instead.
-   **Guilty**: Knows he hurt Ruckus/Dogs. Trying to atone.

## Combat Style (Tier 1 - Elite)
-   **Bare-Knuckle Boxing**: Trained since age 12. Fast, brutal, precise.
-   **Dirty Fighter**: Knows how to win at any cost.

## Current Status (Feb 2026)
-   **Conditional**: Living in The Kennel. Probationary member.
-   **Hunted**: West End wants him dead (traitor).
-   **Ruckus**: Open hostility. Trying to avoid him.
-   **Stray**: His mentor/savior. Follows him like a shadow.

## Relationships
-   **Stray**: Saved his life. Gave him his coat. Radical loyalty.
-   **Ruckus**: Mortal enemy (hospitalized him). Seeking forgiveness.
-   **Dame**: Wary but hopeful.
-   **West End Leader**: Former mentor/father figure. Now wants him dead.
